Testimonials

Jake Sumrall

VP, Sumrall Oil Services

“We really depend on having somebody that we can trust. We found that with Joe Barlow.”

— Jake Sumrall, VP, Sumrall Oil Services

Would you tell us a little about yourself and your company?

I'm Jake Sumrall, Vice President of Sumrall Oil Services. We are mainly a fuel distributor for convenience stores and gas stations. We also have a few stores that we operate ourselves. We are not large enough to have really a contractor or mechanic or anything like that on hand. So, we are dependent on outsourcing.

How did your relationship with Joe Barlow start?

We met Joe when we a got a quote a few years ago. It didn't work out for that particular project, but we started giving him calls after that and he's just been great to work with since then. I don't know a lot about construction or repair work. We really depend on having somebody that we can trust. We found that with Joe Barlow.

Does Joe handle things in a timely way? Do you feel he has a sense of urgency?

I’ve called him late in the afternoon, or at night. I'll send a message or an email. I'll have an email the next morning that came in at like 11 or 12 that night that he's already taken care of it. So yeah, anytime we've had an issue pop up, he's always been quick to respond and get it resolved.

Did you say you had an example of the kind of person Joe is?

Yes, in 2019, after Mississippi State won the Egg Bowl, they were taking the trophy around to different sites and they happen to choose one of our stores in Madison. We only had like 36 hours’ notice. Somebody told Joe about it and asked him to tell a few people. I don't know exactly what Joe did but when we got there, cars were everywhere. I think it was the biggest turn-outs they had for any of those events. I think he had a lot to do with it, which really looked good for the store and for the company.
